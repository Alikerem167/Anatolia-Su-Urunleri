Our History

1981

Five brothers, shoulder to shoulder, took the first step in Istanbul Kumkapı fish market under the name Sağdıçlar Balıkçılık.

1985

The first local cultured fish was sold.

2004

The first fresh fish counter was opened.

2005

İdeal Hipermarkets were established. It started to operate in an area of 400 m2 and started to serve the people of the region.

2006

Balık Ye Restaurant started its service in Çekmeköy. For the first time in Turkiye, Balık Ye Restaurant, which was launched to offer cooked seafood to homes, offers the most delicious and fresh fish dishes to its customers.

2011

SaveAs Software was founded. Our company develops and supports ERP, HRM, CRM, e-commerce sites and mobile apps.

2016

Production Facility was opened in Istanbul. In a 1200 m2 closed area, production started with slaughterhouse, heat treatment, smoked, frozen product and shrimp lines.

2018

Balıkye ve BandoE-commerce was started with BalıkYe.com. It started daily fresh fish and frozen product deliveries to its customers all over Istanbul.

2019

Turkish Salmon farm started production in Artvin Arhavi.

2021

A alevin trout farm was established in Gümüşhane Kürtün Dam.

2022

Trabzon fish processing plant started activities. In a total area of 8000 m2 including 3200 m2 closed area, the processing process of Turkish Salmon harvested from our farms has started.

About Us

Sağdıçlar Group is a group of companies that exports to many countries of the world today, starting from a modest business that five brothers started shoulder to shoulder in Istanbul Yenikapı Fish Market in the early 1980s, and operates in different sectors such as wholesale and retail food, restaurant, IT, and construction, especially fishery.
