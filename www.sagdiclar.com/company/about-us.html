About Us

Sağdıçlar Group is a group of companies that exports to many countries of the world today, starting from a modest business that five brothers started shoulder to shoulder in Istanbul Yenikapı Fish Market in the early 1980s, and operates in different sectors such as wholesale and retail food, restaurant, IT, and construction, especially fishery.

Sağdıçlar Balıkçılık, the first and largest of the group companies, is one of the leading companies in the fish and seafood sector in Turkiye. Sağdıçlar continues its activities in this field with domestic and international wholesale and retail fresh fish trade, aquaculture, processed seafood production and fish restaurants. Since its establishment, the company has been supplying seafood to many private enterprises, especially markets, restaurants, catering, touristic and social facilities in the homeland, in addition to its sales points in Izmir and Istanbul.

Sağdıçlar started aquaculture activities by establishing Turkish Salmon farms with an annual capacity of 4000 tonnes in Artvin in 2019 under the roof of Ideal Balıkçılık company. In the following years, it established alevin farms first in Gümüşhane and then in Artvin. Sağdıçlar has managed to deliver its products to many different countries of the world with its Turkish Salmon farming activities.

Balık Ye started out as the brand of the group's fish restaurants in the first stage, and its first branch started its operations in Çekmeköy in 2006. The Balık Ye brand has also been included under the group structure as the group's processed seafood, fresh produce, and franchise restaurant brand. At the same time, they meet with their customers via balikye.com as an online fish market. Production, wholesale and retail trade of cultured fish, sea fish and processed seafood is carried out under the Bando brand.

Bando branded products have taken their place on the shelves of market chains in many countries. Sağdıçlar, which serves both producers and consumers at many stages of the fisheries sector, continues to invest and grow in line with the needs and demands of its customers with its innovative, sustainable, and efficient production vision.

Save As Software was established in 2011 to meet the technological software and hardware needs of group companies. All of the group companies use software developed by Save As. Save As Software continues its growth by adding many corporate brands other than group companies to its customer portfolio.

İdeal Hipermarket is the first company operating outside of fisheries within the Group. In 2005, Ideal Hipermarket, which started its activities with its first branch in Çekmeköy, continues to operate in many districts on the Anatolian Side of Istanbul with its new branches over time, especially ideal.com.tr online market.

Sağdıçlar Group, which increases its strength both in its country and globally every day, continues to fulfil its duties and responsibilities by taking its place in the global market with its experienced history in the aquaculture and food sectors, strong brands, and advanced software technologies, and continues to contribute to human health and the national economy.
